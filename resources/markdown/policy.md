# Campus Rental Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

- Account data: name, email, student details, and profile information.
- Listing and rental data: items, requests, approvals, and status history.
- Security data: login activity, verification status, and session information.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use data to:

- Provide marketplace features and user authentication.
- Support rental transactions between users.
- Improve security, prevent abuse, and enforce platform rules.
- Send transactional notifications related to rentals and account actions.

## 3. Data Sharing

We share information only as necessary:

- With other users when needed for rental coordination.
- With authorized campus administrators for moderation and support.
- When required by law or school policy.

We do not sell personal data.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain account and transaction records for operational, security, and compliance purposes. Retention periods may depend on campus policy and legal requirements.

## 5. Security

We use reasonable safeguards for account and platform security. No online system is 100 percent secure, so users should also protect their credentials and devices.

## 6. Your Choices

You may update profile information from your account settings. You may request account deletion, subject to records required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

## 7. Cookies and Sessions

Campus Rental uses essential cookies and session tools to keep you signed in, secure your account, and support application functionality.

## 8. Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised version.

## 9. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact your campus platform administrator.
